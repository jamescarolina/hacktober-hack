WELCOME TO St. Joseph's Boys' Higher Secondary School

This is a Jesuits institution.The school was established in the year 1792.
Now about 1500 students are studying here.School has a full fledged physics, chemistry, botany, zoology and computer lab with internet facility.
All the classes are provided with smart class facility for the students to experience the latest technological teaching experience.
For +1 and +2 the school has 4 combinations.

  • SCIENCE - Physics,Chemistry,Mathematics,Biology.
  • COMPUTER SCIENCE - Physics,Chemistry,Mathematics,Computer Science.
  • COMMERCE - Buisness Studies,Accountancy,Economics,Computer Application.
  • HUMANITIES - Sociology,History,Economics,Political Science.
